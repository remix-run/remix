# Claude Guide

Follow the repository guidance in `AGENTS.md`.

When contributing to GitHub, do not identify, credit, or attribute work to Claude or any other AI or agentic tool. Do not add `Generated by`, `Co-authored-by`, tool branding, signatures, or similar attribution to commit messages, pull request titles or bodies, issue comments, release notes, or other GitHub-facing content. Present the work transparently in terms of the changes made and their rationale.
